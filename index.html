Frequently Asked Questions

What is Trade Place Nigeria?
Trade Place Nigeria is an online platform where individuals and businesses can buy and sell a wide range of products and services securely and conveniently.
How does Trade Place Nigeria work?
Sellers can list their products, and buyers can browse, purchase, and arrange delivery or pickup. The platform connects buyers and sellers while ensuring a secure transaction process.
How do I list my products?
After registering as a seller, go to your dashboard, click “Add Product,” upload images, and enter product details like price and description.
How does delivery work?
Buyers can choose between home delivery, express shipping, or pickup from the seller, depending on the seller’s options.
